# Refund and Downtime Compensation Policy

## 1. General Provisions
This Policy governs refunds and downtime (unavailability) compensation for virtual private servers (“**VPS**”) provided by **Amnezia Hosting**.  
Payment for and use of the VPS service constitutes the client’s full acceptance of these terms.

## 2. Refunds

### First month
- The first month’s VPS fee is **non-refundable**.
- In exceptional cases, where a technical failure attributable to Amnezia Hosting’s infrastructure is confirmed, a **partial credit** may be granted at Support’s discretion.

### Refunds for unused periods
- If the service is cancelled **within 30 calendar days** from the activation date, the client is refunded **100% of all full unused months except the first month**.
- **Partially used months** are not refundable.
- Payments for **add-on services** (domains, SSL, IP addresses, licenses, etc.) are **non-refundable**.

### Limitations
- After the 30-day period expires, monetary refunds are **not issued**, except for downtime compensation as provided herein.
- No refund is granted in case of a **breach of the Acceptable Use Policy** by the client or for issues **caused by the client’s actions**.

> By activating the VPS, the client agrees to the **immediate start** of the service and acknowledges that, once activated, the client **waives the 14-day withdrawal right** provided by law.

## 3. Downtime Compensation

### Form of compensation
Compensation is provided as an **extension of the VPS term**, not as a monetary refund.

### Conditions
- Upon a confirmed outage attributable to Amnezia Hosting that **exceeds 4 consecutive hours within a calendar day**, the client is credited with **1 additional day of service**.
- The **maximum compensation** per incident is **30 days**.
- Outages **shorter than 4 hours** are **not compensated**.
- Downtime caused by the **client’s actions** (misconfiguration, OS deletion, system interference, DDoS, etc.) is **not compensated**.

### How to claim
- A request must be submitted via the **Client Area** within **7 days** after the incident.
- After verification and confirmation, the extension is applied within **2 business days**.

## 4. Request Handling
- Requests are processed through the **ticketing system**.
- Review time: **up to 3 business days**.
- Monetary refunds (if applicable) are processed within **5 business days** after approval.
- Extensions are applied within **2 business days** after confirmation.

## 5. Responsibilities
Amnezia Hosting will review requests **in good faith** and **within the stated timeframes**.  
The client must provide **accurate information** and comply with the **Acceptable Use Policy**.  
In cases of abuse (e.g., fabricated incidents), Amnezia Hosting may **deny compensation**.

## 6. Miscellaneous
Amnezia Hosting may **update** this Policy; the current version is published on the website and takes effect **upon publication**.  
Where national or European law affords the client **broader rights**, such rights shall **prevail**.
